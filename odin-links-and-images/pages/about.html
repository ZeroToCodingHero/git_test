About The Odin Project

The Odin Project is a free, open-source curriculum for learning web development. It was created to provide a comprehensive and accessible learning experience for aspiring developers. The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, and more.

The Odin Project emphasizes hands-on learning through projects and exercises, allowing students to build real-world applications as they progress through the curriculum. It also fosters a supportive community where learners can collaborate, ask questions, and share their progress.
